This is where the work becomes usable in real situations.

A mandate lens takes what the practice keeps steady and turns it toward a
particular kind of pressure.

You do not need this page first.

Come here when the practice already makes sense and you want help bringing it
into real work.

### What this section is for

This section helps you see:

- what a mandate lens is trying to make more visible
- which lens you can try right now
- where to go if you want the deeper repository reading later

### Reading and trying

Reading this section is not the same as loading a lens.

These pages help you recognize what a lens is for.

Trying one begins when you bring its text into the conversation you are having
with a model or agent.

In practice, that usually means opening the seeder file, copying its text, and
pasting it into the tool you are using, or loading the same file from a local
checkout.
