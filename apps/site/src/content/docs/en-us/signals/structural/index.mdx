Structural signals are public entries for pressure patterns that need more than
a definition.

They name a pressure pattern, keep the reasoning path visible, and give the
reader a way to test the claim against their own work.

They are not announcements.

They are not release notes.

They are not a feed.

### What belongs here

A structural signal belongs here when it does three things:

1. names a condition people can recognize
2. exposes the assumptions beneath that condition
3. leaves a trace that can be inspected, challenged, or adapted

The essay may begin from AI, delivery, organizational pressure, public practice,
or the way understanding moves between people.

The test is not topic alone.

The test is whether the piece helps a reader see a structure that was already
acting on their work.

Each signal should make a claim, show enough of its path, and keep the boundary
between observation and interpretation visible.

If a piece points toward practice, the activation belongs after the reader has
enough context to judge whether the practice fits.

### AI & Cognitive Amplification

The first structural signal cluster begins with:
<a href={ESSAY_HREF}>AI Is Not Magic. It Is a Cognitive Amplifier.</a>

It begins from the public noise around AI and argues that AI works as a
cognitive amplifier: scaling reasoning-like outputs inside institutions already
shaped by power.

That first piece is the map, not the whole terrain. Later signals in this
cluster can deepen the questions it opens: who gets the time back, who supplied
the work and memory, where the machine touches the ground, what safety means
beyond a filter, how public services can be challenged, who writes the rules,
and why the reasoning layer must remain inspectable.

The sequence should stay with continuous public practice: trace, source
boundaries, provisional trust, and reasoning that can be inspected and revised.
