<RegisterContent register="orientation/en-us">
  AI-assisted work now lets teams generate ideas, code, and decisions faster than they can fully understand them. When output outpaces understanding, coordination gets harder, review gets heavier, and trust starts to thin out.

  Practice of Clarity is a publication about that condition and about a usable response to it: making reasoning visible so shared understanding can form without pretending certainty.
