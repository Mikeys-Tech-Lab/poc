You do not need this page first.

It is here for when you want a map of how the public writing, mandate lenses,
context seeders, and public node relate.

### The parts

**Public writing**  
Where the condition and the practice can be entered.

**Seeds**  
Where the core distinctions of the practice are held in stable form.

**Mandate lenses**  
Where the practice becomes specific to a kind of work.

**Context seeders**  
Where a lens is brought into a working environment.

**Public node**  
Where the work can be inspected, evolved further, and made visible over time.  
One example is the [repository](https://github.com/Mikeys-Tech-Lab/poc), but a node can take different forms depending on where the practice is carried.

### What each part does

#### Public writing

It is where the condition, the language, and the practice can be encountered without requiring anything from the reader yet. Its job is not activation. Its job is orientation, recognition, and enough clarity for the work to become legible.

#### Seeds

They hold the core distinctions the practice depends on.

They are not tied to a single context and do not activate anything on their own. Their role is to keep the language and boundaries of the practice stable as the work moves into different situations.

#### Mandate lenses

They take what is stable in the practice and bring it toward a particular kind of work. They do not replace the public writing, and they are not the foundation. They are where the practice starts becoming usable inside a real situation.

#### Context seeders

They bring selected parts of the work into a live reasoning environment. That
is when the practice stops being only something you read and starts shaping how
claims are formed, checked, and revised.

#### Public node

It holds the materials and the visible history of how the work changes over time. It is where the architecture can be traced more fully, not because the repo is the practice, but because it makes the evolution of the work inspectable.

The repository is one place where this work is made visible. It is not the only
place it can exist.

These parts are connected, but they do different work.

Public writing is for reading. Mandate lenses are for application. Context seeders are for activation. The public node is for inspection, evolution, and trace.
