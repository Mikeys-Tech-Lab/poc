**Mikey Sebastian Drozd** <sup>he/him · they/them</sup>

I work as a consultant at Thoughtworks and as a structural practitioner focused on AI-augmented delivery, decision hygiene, and organisational coherence under pressure.

This publication surface for the Practice of Clarity did not begin as a publication or a formal framework. It emerged from years of trying to understand why capable teams repeatedly lose shared understanding even when people are acting in good faith.

## How this work emerged

Since around 2019, I have been moving through a long process of unlearning. That meant examining how reasoning is shaped by institutions, incentives, inherited narratives, and professional habits that become normal. It also meant refusing forms of performance that reward certainty over honesty, extraction over clarity, and speed over understanding.

What began as delivery experience evolved into a structural lens: situated, globally conscious, and aware of its own limits. From 2019 through late 2025, I spent years tracing how systems shape decision behavior, how institutions normalize drift, and how shared reasoning breaks down under pressure.

The arrival of powerful AI systems intensified this inquiry. Generative tools can produce persuasive output while hallucinating reasoning. That made one thing unmistakable: teams can very easily drift into performance instead of understanding.

The Practice of Clarity emerged as a response. It is a way of making reasoning visible while work is still unfolding, so decisions remain examinable, uncertainty stays speakable, and responsibility can be located without pretending certainty.

## Current practice

Since late 2025, this practice has been applied and refined while enabling a high-performance engineering team operating inside an AI-accelerated delivery environment. The public writing, the public node, and the texts used to bring the work into practice show it in action while it continues to evolve.

This work continues through others wherever it is taken up and practiced.

This perspective is also shaped by Slavic roots and queer life. These experiences inform the lens of the work without exhausting it.

<StillEvolvingNote>

The writing on this site is authored by a person. AI may assist with drafting and refinement inside disciplined practice, but judgment, responsibility, and authorship remain human.
