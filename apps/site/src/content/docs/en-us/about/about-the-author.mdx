**Mikey Sebastian Drozd** (he/him, they/them).

The Practice of Clarity began as a way to make reasoning visible in environments where speed often outpaces understanding. It grew from years of working across technology, design, and organisational decision-making, where the same pattern kept appearing: people making good-faith decisions with incomplete shared context, then spending significant effort repairing the drift.

This site is the publication surface for that practice. The writing is done by a person, not generated. The structure is deliberate. The repository and implementation details will be published later as a demonstration of the practice itself.
