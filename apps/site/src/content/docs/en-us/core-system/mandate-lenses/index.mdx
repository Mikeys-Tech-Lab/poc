This is where the work becomes usable in real situations.

A mandate lens takes what the practice keeps steady and turns it toward a specific kind of pressure.

It helps when the practice already makes sense and you want support bringing it
into real work.

A mandate lens makes three things easier to see:

- what a mandate lens is trying to make more visible
- which lens you can try right now
- where the deeper source text lives when you want to inspect it

### Reading and trying

Reading about a lens is not the same as loading it.

These pages help you recognize what a lens is for.

Trying one begins when you bring its text into a conversation with a model or agent.

In practice, that usually means opening the seeder file, copying its text, and
pasting it into the tool you are using, or loading the same file from a local
checkout.
