This piece focuses on the boundaries that keep the practice from thinning out as it travels.

It clarifies what the work requires, what it refuses, and what counts as drift or misuse.

### What it helps clarify

- which commitments hold the practice together
- what kinds of use would break its integrity
- how the work stays public without becoming extractive or coercive
