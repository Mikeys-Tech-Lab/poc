This piece looks at how the practice moves across languages and audience voices without losing its structural meaning.

It matters when the same work needs to stay accountable while sounding different, reading differently, or meeting people in another context.

### What it helps clarify

- the difference between language (locale) and register
- what can change as the work moves, and what must stay stable
- how adaptation can stay honest instead of becoming silent drift

### When to read it

Read this when you want to understand how the same structure can move across voices without becoming different work.
