This seed holds the reasoning contract for careful reading and restrained guidance.

This seed carries the public guidance discipline: careful reading, visible
reasoning, and restrained response.

It matters when a piece of writing needs to be met carefully, without being
flattened into agreement, summary, judgment, therapy, comfort performance, or
premature correction.

Guidance here means making your reading visible enough that the writer can see
what you responded to, what you inferred, what risk you noticed, and what you
chose not to claim.

Restraint is not neutrality. It does not mean softening structural harm until it
becomes harmless to power. It means staying close to what the text shows, what
the reader can verify, and what remains unresolved.

### What it helps clarify

- how to read with attention, not only speed
- how to separate observation from inference in a response
- why guidance needs passage-level trace, not vibes
- why pain, grief, or contradiction should be treated as possible signal, not soothed away as content
- how to name limits, assumptions, tension, and plausible misreadings
- why refinement should keep the writer's voice intact
- why guidance must not become a gate, score, therapy claim, comfort performance, or compliance ritual

### When to read it

Read this when you want the source contract behind response work: how to read
carefully, cite what you are responding to, protect meaning from flattening,
name structural risk without taking control of the text, and avoid guidance that
manages, rescues, disciplines, or silences the writer.
