Seeds are deeper writings that help the work keep its shape as it moves.

They stay with questions the public pages do not need to carry all at once. More will come over time.

You do not need them first.

They are here for when you want to go deeper, not before you begin.

### What this section is for

This section is not the first thing to read.

Come here when you want more depth, grounding, or the writing the public pages point toward.
