This reading holds the posture of response.

It matters when a piece of writing needs to be met carefully, not flattened into
agreement, summary, or premature correction.

### What it helps clarify

- how to read with attention instead of speed alone
- how response can stay honest about limits, assumptions, and tension
- why guidance needs restraint as much as precision

### When to read it

Read this when you want a deeper sense of how the practice sounds when it is
being carried well in conversation or guidance.
