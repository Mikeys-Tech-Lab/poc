This writing focuses on the boundaries that keep the practice from thinning out
as it travels.

It is where the work becomes clearer about what it requires, what it refuses,
and what would count as drift or misuse.

### What it helps clarify

- which commitments hold the practice together
- what kinds of use would break its integrity
- how the work stays public without becoming extractive or coercive
