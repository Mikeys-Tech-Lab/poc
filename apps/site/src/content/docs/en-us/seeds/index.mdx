These are deeper readings that hold parts of the work steady.

They stay with questions the public pages do not need to carry all at once.

They help the work keep its shape as it moves across contexts. More will come
over time.

You do not need them first.

They are here for when you want to go deeper, not before you begin.

### What this section is for

This section is not the first thing to read.

Come here when you want more depth, more grounding, or more of the writing that
the public pages only point toward.
