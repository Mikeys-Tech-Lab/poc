These are interconnected reasoning seeds.

They were written to help reasoning stay inspectable, bounded, and coherent as
the practice moves across contexts.

They matter especially in contexts where reasoning-capable models are in use,
but they are not limited to those contexts.

You do not need them first.

They are here for when you want to go deeper into how the work holds together.
More seeds will come over time.

### What this section is for

This section is not the entry point.

It is where you go when you want more depth, more grounding, or a clearer sense
of the writings that help reasoning hold its shape as the practice moves.
