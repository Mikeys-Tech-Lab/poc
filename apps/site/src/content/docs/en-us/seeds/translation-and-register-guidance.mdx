This piece stays with how the practice moves across languages and across
audience voices without losing its structural meaning.

It matters when the same work needs to remain accountable while sounding
different, reading differently, or meeting people in another context.

### What it helps clarify

- the difference between locale and register
- what can change as the work moves, and what must stay stable
- how adaptation can stay honest instead of becoming silent drift

### When to read it

Read this when you want to understand why the same structure can be carried in
different voices without becoming different work.
