## What this is

This site is a place to **read** the Practice of Clarity.

The goal is simple: help people explain their thinking in a way that other people can follow.

Sometimes life moves fast. People make decisions quickly. And then everyone ends up with different stories about what is happening and why. This practice is about making the “why” visible.

## How the buttons work

You will see three controls in the top-right (and in the mobile menu):

- **Language**: switch between locale versions (en‑US, en‑GB, de‑DE).
- **Register**: switch between two writing styles:
  - **Practitioner**: written for people comfortable with technical and organisational language.
  - **Beginner**: written in everyday language.
- **Theme**: change how the site looks (dark/light/solid). Pick what feels easiest to read.
