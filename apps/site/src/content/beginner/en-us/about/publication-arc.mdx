## Publication arc

This practice is shared in stages.

The simple idea is: first explain the practice clearly, then later share the “behind the scenes” implementation.

## The stages

- **Prologue**: what this practice is, and why it exists.
- **Act I**: the problem this practice responds to (things moving fast, people losing track).
- **Act II**: the discipline (show your reasoning, not just your conclusion).
- **Act III**: how it can scale without one person being “the boss” of meaning.
- **Act IV**: the repository is released as a real example of the practice in action.

For now, this website is the reading surface. The implementation is published later on purpose.
