This page explains the licence for the **writing** on this site.

## In plain language

You are allowed to:

- share it (send it to people, copy it, post it elsewhere)
- change it (adapt it, translate it, reuse parts of it)

Even for commercial use.

You just need to:

- give credit to the author
- link to the licence
- say if you changed anything
