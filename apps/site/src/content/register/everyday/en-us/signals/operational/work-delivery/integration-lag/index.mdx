Integration Lag is a short series about one thing: work can move faster than
people can understand it together.

It is not about blaming speed, and it is not about blaming AI. It stays with
what happens when work goes out before everyone has caught up.

### What the series covers

There are three parts, and they follow each other:

1. [We Started Shipping Faster. We Also Started Understanding Less.](/en-us/signals/operational/work-delivery/integration-lag/we-started-shipping-faster-understanding-less/) names the gap, and shows how AI can make unfinished work look done.
2. [A Path Through Integration Lag](/en-us/signals/operational/work-delivery/integration-lag/a-path-through-integration-lag/) is about keeping understanding close to the work.
3. [The Verification Tax](/en-us/signals/operational/work-delivery/integration-lag/the-verification-tax/) is about the checking that still has to happen afterward.

Read them in order, or start with the one that feels closest to what you are
seeing.
