This section is about work getting faster.

Software gets built and shipped more quickly now, and AI is part of why. But
speed is not the whole story.

AI shows up here in a few ways. People use it as a tool to get work done.
People also end up working under it. And people meet it as users, often without
choosing to.

### What you will find here

These signals name things that are easy to feel but hard to say at work:

- output going out faster than people can keep up
- a tool changing the job before anyone agrees what changed
- extra checking that no one really counts

Start with the part that feels familiar. Then follow the signal far enough to
see if it gives you words you can use with other people.

### Integration Lag

The first set of signals here is Integration Lag. It follows one story in three
steps: work speeds up, understanding falls behind, and someone still has to
check the result.
