This reading makes room for not knowing yet without treating it as failure.

It shows how uncertainty can stay speakable, structured, and serious instead of being rushed into false clarity.

### What it helps you see

- how uncertainty can still be held with discipline
- why openness is not the same as vagueness
- how you can stay honest without giving up judgment

### When to read it

Read this when you want help staying open under pressure, especially when the surrounding culture rewards fast confidence.
