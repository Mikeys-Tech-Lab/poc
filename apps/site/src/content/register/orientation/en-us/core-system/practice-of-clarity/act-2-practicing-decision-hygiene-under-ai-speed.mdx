Work can now move very quickly.

This is not only because of AI.

Work has been moving faster for a long time.

AI makes it easier to produce answers that look complete before they are fully understood.

When things move faster, it becomes easier to lose track of why decisions were made.

This is where a different way of working becomes important.

### The idea

Instead of focusing only on the result, teams also keep track of:

- what they are assuming
- what they are unsure about
- what might change later

This is called decision hygiene.

It means keeping the thinking visible while work is still happening.

The work is no longer only the artifact.
It also includes the assumptions behind it and the uncertainty that remains.

Without this, teams often keep going even when something is unclear.

Questions get skipped because there is no time.  
People agree so things keep moving.

The work continues.

Understanding falls behind.

### What changes

Teams begin to ask small questions during the work:

- what are we assuming here?
- what do we not know yet?
- where could this drift?

These are not formal steps or checkpoints.

They are part of how work moves forward.

This creates a tension that cannot be solved by rules or policies.

The thinking needs to be visible so people can understand each other, but it should not turn into something people perform or report.

If it becomes something to complete or prove, people will focus on looking clear instead of being clear.

This way of working cannot be forced from the outside.

It has to happen inside the work itself.

### What this is not

<ReadingFrame variant="boundary" label="Boundary statement">
  <p>This is not about more process or more documentation.</p>
  <p>
    It may feel different at first, because things that were implicit are now
    spoken out loud.
  </p>
  <p>
    Over time, it often reduces confusion, even if the path there is not
    always smooth.
  </p>
  <p>It should make work clearer, not slower.</p>
  <p>It is also not about proving that you are right.</p>
  <ReadingBeat>
    If teams are asked to "show their reasoning" as a deliverable, it becomes
    performance.
  </ReadingBeat>
  <p>
    If it becomes something to complete or measure, people will focus on
    looking clear instead of being clear.
  </p>
</ReadingFrame>

The purpose is not to record everything.

The purpose is to make the right things visible at the right moment.
