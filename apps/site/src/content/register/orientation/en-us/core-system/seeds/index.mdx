Seeds are reasoning-contract texts that help the work keep its shape as it moves.

People can read them to understand what the practice requires. AI systems can
load them as part of a source context, so responses are shaped by the same
constraints.

They stay with questions the public pages cannot carry all at once.

Return here when you want more grounding, clearer language, or more of the
reasoning that holds the practice together.
