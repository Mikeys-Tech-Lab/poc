title: Mandate Lenses
---

Mandate lenses are the part of the system that turns the practice into something
you can use in a real context.

They are not the seed layer and they are not the continuity layer.

They are the applied packages.

### What this section is for

This section helps you see:

- what a mandate lens is
- which lens exists right now
- where the real source lives

### Important boundary

These pages can introduce a lens.

They do not activate it.

A lens only becomes active when its repository artifacts are loaded.

### Package shape

Each lens package contains:

- `README.md` for entry and notes
- `lens.md` for the canonical lens
- `context-seeder.md` for the portable loading form

See the root family package:

- [mandateLenses/README.md](https://github.com/Mikeys-Tech-Lab/poc/blob/main/mandateLenses/README.md)
