This is where the practice starts becoming usable in real situations.

A mandate lens helps when the practice already makes sense, but you want help
bringing it into a specific kind of pressure.

You do not need the repository first.

### What this section is for

This section helps you see:

- what a mandate lens helps make visible
- which lens you can try right now
- where the deeper repository reading lives, if you want it later

### Important boundary

These pages can introduce a lens.

They do not activate it.

A lens starts being used when you bring its text into the conversation you are
having with a model or agent.

In practice, that usually means opening the seeder file, copying its text, and
pasting it into the tool you are using, or loading the same file from a local
checkout.

If that feels technical, keep it simple: open the seeder link, copy the text,
and bring it into the tool you are already using.
