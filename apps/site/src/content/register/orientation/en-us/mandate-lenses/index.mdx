This is where the practice becomes usable in real situations.

A mandate lens helps when the practice makes sense, but you need support bringing it into a specific kind of pressure.

You do not need the repository first.

### What this section is for

This section helps you see:

- what a lens makes visible in practice
- which lens you can try right now
- where to go if you want to read deeper later

### Important boundary

These pages introduce a lens.

They do not activate it.

A lens starts being used when you bring its text into the conversation you are having with a model or agent.

In practice, this usually means opening the seeder link, copying the text, and bringing it into the tool you are already using.
