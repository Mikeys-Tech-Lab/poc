Integration Lag is a short series about one situation: work can move faster than
the shared understanding it depends on.

It names that situation and stays with it. The series does not blame speed or
blame AI. It follows what happens to understanding when work ships before people
have caught up.

### What the series covers

The three signals build on each other:

1. [We Started Shipping Faster. We Also Started Understanding Less.](/en-us/signals/operational/work-delivery/integration-lag/we-started-shipping-faster-understanding-less/) names integration lag and shows how AI can make unfinished thinking look complete.
2. [A Path Through Integration Lag](/en-us/signals/operational/work-delivery/integration-lag/a-path-through-integration-lag/) moves from naming the gap to keeping understanding attached to the work.
3. [The Verification Tax](/en-us/signals/operational/work-delivery/integration-lag/the-verification-tax/) stays with the checking and correcting that remains once drafts arrive faster than confidence.

Read them in order, or start with the one that matches what you already
recognize.
