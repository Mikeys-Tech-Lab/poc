Structural signals help a reader slow down and see what is happening
underneath a familiar pressure.

The pressure may come from AI, delivery speed, unclear decisions, or work that
looks complete before people understand it together.

### What these essays do

Each signal should help you:

1. recognize the situation
2. see the structure underneath it
3. understand the claim being made
4. decide whether the practice fits your context

The goal is not to persuade you quickly.

The goal is to make the path clear enough that you can follow it, question it,
or adapt it.

Start with the situation the signal names.

Then look for the distinction it offers.

If it points to a practice, treat that practice as something to test in a real
setting, not something to adopt because the page says so.

### AI & Cognitive Amplification

The first structural signal cluster begins with:
<a href={buildEssayHref('orientation')}>AI Is Not Magic. It Is a Cognitive Amplifier.</a>

It argues that AI works as a cognitive amplifier, then asks who controls that
amplification and what record is left for people affected by it.

That piece works as the opening map. Later signals can deepen the map without
overloading the first essay: labor and time, extraction and memory,
infrastructure cost, safety and trace, public services, regulation, and the
reasoning layer as public infrastructure.

The sequence should stay grounded in public practice, trace, and revision.
