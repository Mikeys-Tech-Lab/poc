The pressure is not only that there is more output.

It is that work can move faster than people can make sense of it together.

Practice of Clarity responds to that condition. It keeps the thinking visible so people can stay oriented while decisions are still being made.

### What this is

This is a practice, not a product or a protocol.

It is a way of working that keeps judgment inspectable instead of letting it disappear into speed.

It asks people and machine-assisted work alike to show how conclusions were reached.

### What this changes

The point is not better-sounding answers.

The point is to make assumptions, uncertainty, and decision paths visible enough to question and revise.

That means:

- naming assumptions
- separating observation from inference
- keeping uncertainty visible
- leaving a trail that can be checked later

### What this is not

This is not:

- surveillance
- forced reporting
- productivity theater
- pressure to sound certain when you are not
- one-way transparency without protection

If visible reasoning becomes another burden or another way to control people, it is no longer this practice.

### Reading and operating are different

This page explains the practice.

It does not activate it.

Reading can help you understand the work. It does not load the posture the work depends on.

That only begins when the first mandate lens is loaded through its context seeder.
