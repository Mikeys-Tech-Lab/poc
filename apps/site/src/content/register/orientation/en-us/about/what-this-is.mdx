This site is a **publication surface** for the Practice of Clarity.

The basic idea is simple: make reasoning visible so people can better understand how decisions were formed, what is still uncertain, and where the pressure is landing.

## Reading this site

You can adjust the site in three ways, depending on how you want to read.

### Register

The site title shows the current register: **Practitioner** or **Orientation**. Click the title to switch.

Practitioner uses more technical and organisational language. Orientation explains the same ideas in clearer everyday language, with a gentler pace and more direct explanation. The structure stays the same. Nothing is hidden. Only the voice changes.

### Theme

The theme selector lets you change how the site looks. You can choose between atmospheric and solid styles, in both dark and light versions. This changes comfort and readability, not meaning.

The accessibility button opens a panel with extra reading controls.

It currently includes:

- **Increase contrast**
- **Text size**
- **Reduce motion**
- **Solid background for higher contrast**
- **Underline links**
- **Alternate font**
- **Reset to defaults**

These settings do not change the content. They are there so different people can read in ways that better fit their sensory, visual, and cognitive needs.

## What you can expect

This is a **small launch surface**.

Right now it includes:

- the core explanation pages
- the first essay in Practice of Clarity
- the public license pages
- a visible look at the infrastructure behind the publication

The technical side already exists. This site simply keeps the writing at the center while the practice is introduced step by step.

## What this is not

This is not:

- surveillance
- forced reporting about yourself
- productivity performance
- pressure to sound certain when you are not
- one-way transparency where people below are exposed and people above stay protected

Making reasoning visible should reduce confusion and help people share context. If it turns into another burden or another way to control people, it stops being this practice.
