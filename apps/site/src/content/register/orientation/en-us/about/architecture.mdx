This page is a map. You do not need it to begin.

This work holds together through a few distinct parts. They are related, but they do not do the same job.

### The parts

- Public writing: where the condition and the practice can be entered
- Mandate lenses: where the practice becomes specific to a kind of work
- Context seeders: where a lens is brought into a working environment
- Public node (repo): where the work can be inspected and evolved further

### How they relate

Most people begin with Act I or a public page.

From there, a mandate lens can be used to bring the practice into real work. `Sensible Defaults` is the first lens for that.

When a context seeder is used, it shapes how reasoning happens in that environment and leaves a trace that can be followed.

If you want to go deeper, the public node contains the fuller structure of how this work is held and evolves.
