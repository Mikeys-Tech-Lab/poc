**Mikey Sebastian Drozd** <sup>he/him · they/them</sup>

This site is where the Practice of Clarity is written down and shared. The practice itself grew from years of trying to understand why people can be capable, careful, and committed, and still end up in confusion, rework, and drift.

## How this work emerged

Since around 2019, I have been unlearning how certainty is produced. That meant looking at how institutions shape thinking, how pressure rewards fast answers over honest ones, and how people end up repeating patterns they never chose.

Over time, the focus shifted. Less on opinions, more on structure. Less on appearing certain, more on staying accountable to what is known and what is not. This work grew out of tracing how decisions move, where they break, and why shared understanding disappears under speed.

When AI tools entered everyday work, this became harder to ignore. They can produce confident answers without showing how they were formed. That makes it easy to mistake output for understanding.

The Practice of Clarity comes out of that tension. It is a way to keep reasoning visible while work is still moving, so decisions can be followed, questioned, and understood.

## Current practice

Since late 2025, this work has been tested in environments where AI is part of daily delivery. The writing you see here reflects that practice in motion. It is not a finished system. It is something being worked on in public.

Wherever others take it up, the practice continues in different forms.

My background includes Slavic roots and queer life. These shape how I see power, language, and survival, without defining the limits of the work.
