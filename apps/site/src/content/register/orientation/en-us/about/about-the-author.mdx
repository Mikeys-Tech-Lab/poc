**Mikey Sebastian Drozd** <sup>he/him · they/them</sup>

This site is where the Practice of Clarity is written down and shared. The practice itself grew from years of trying to understand why people can be capable, careful, and committed, and still end up in confusion, rework, and drift.

## How this work emerged

Since around 2019, I have been going through a long process of unlearning. That meant looking closely at how institutions shape thinking, how pressure creates performative certainty, and how people often repeat systems they never consciously chose.

Over time, this changed the lens itself. The work became more structural, more globally conscious, and more honest about ignorance. Before late 2025, I spent years tracing how decisions move through organisations, how power shapes behavior, and how shared understanding gets lost under urgency.

The rise of AI tools made this even sharper. These systems can generate convincing answers while inventing reasoning behind the scenes. That made one thing clear: work can easily become performance instead of understanding.

Out of that came the Practice of Clarity: a way to make reasoning easier to see while work is happening, so people can understand why decisions were made, what assumptions are in play, and where uncertainty still lives.

## Current practice

Since late 2025, these ideas have been explored and stress-tested inside an AI-accelerated delivery environment. The public writing, the repository surface, and the seeding artifacts now work together as one usable system under active refinement.

My background includes Slavic roots and queer life. These experiences shape the perspective of the work without becoming the whole story.
