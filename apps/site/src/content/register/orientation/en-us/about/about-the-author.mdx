**Mikey Sebastian Drozd** (he/him, they/them).

The Practice of Clarity started because of a simple problem: people often make decisions quickly, and then spend a lot of time fixing misunderstandings that came from not explaining their reasoning clearly enough.

This site is where that practice gets published. Everything here is written by a person. The structure is intentional. The repository already exists as part of the same work, but this site keeps the writing in the foreground.
