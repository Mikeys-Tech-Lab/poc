This reading helps make room for not knowing yet without treating that as
failure.

It is about how uncertainty can stay speakable, structured, and serious instead
of being rushed into fake clarity.

### What it helps you see

- how uncertainty can still be disciplined
- why openness is not the same as vagueness
- how a person can stay honest without giving up judgment

### When to read it

Read this when you want help staying open under pressure, especially when the
surrounding culture keeps rewarding fast confidence.
