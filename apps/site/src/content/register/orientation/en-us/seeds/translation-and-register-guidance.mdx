This reading helps explain how the work can sound different and still remain
itself.

That matters whenever the practice moves across languages, levels of formality,
or different kinds of readers.

### What it helps you see

- why language and register are not the same thing
- what can shift as the work moves into another voice
- what needs to stay steady so meaning does not drift

### When to read it

Read this when you want to understand how adaptation can stay careful, rather
than quietly altering the structure underneath the words.
