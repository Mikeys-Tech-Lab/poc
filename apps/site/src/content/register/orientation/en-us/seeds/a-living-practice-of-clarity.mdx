This reading helps explain why this practice does not stop at having an answer.

It stays with how the answer was formed, what it leaves out, and whether other
people can still question it together.

### What it helps you see

- why trace matters more than polish
- why clarity is something you do, not something you perform
- why fast output can still leave people less able to understand together

practice, and why the work keeps returning to visible reasoning.
