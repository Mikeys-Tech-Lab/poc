Seeds are deeper writings that help the work keep its shape as it moves.

They stay with questions the public pages do not need to carry all at once.

They keep the work steady across contexts.

You do not need them before you begin.

Come here when you want more grounding, more language, or more of the writing that holds the practice together.

### What this section is for

This is not the first thing to read.

Come here when you want to stay with the work longer, or follow what the public pages only point toward.
