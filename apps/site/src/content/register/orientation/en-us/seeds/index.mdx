These are deeper readings that hold parts of the work steady.

They stay with questions the public pages do not need to carry all at once.

They help the work keep its shape as it moves across contexts. More will come
over time.

You do not need them before you begin.

Come here when you want more grounding, more language, or more of the writing
that helps the practice keep its shape.

### What this section is for

This is not the first thing to read.

Come here when you want to stay with the work longer, or read more of what the
public pages only point toward.
