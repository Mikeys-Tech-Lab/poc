This reading is about what happens when different readings or nodes do not fit
together neatly.

Instead of pretending the tension is already solved, it asks how connection can
remain possible without erasing the difference.

### What it helps you see

- why contradiction can reveal something important
- how a bridge keeps relation without forcing agreement
- what it means to stay connected across real divergence

### When to read it

Read this when the same work is being interpreted in different ways and you do
not want clarity to turn into flattening.
