This reading looks at what happens when different readings or nodes do not fit together.

Instead of smoothing the tension away, it asks how connection can remain without erasing the difference.

### What it helps you see

- why contradiction can point to something that matters
- how a bridge keeps relation without forcing agreement
- what it means to stay connected across real divergence

### When to read it

Read this when the same work is being read in different ways and you do not want clarity to turn into flattening.
