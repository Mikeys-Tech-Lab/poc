This reading names the boundaries that keep the work recognizable as it moves.

It makes clearer what the practice is committed to, and what would make it stop being itself.

### What it helps you see

- what the practice depends on
- what kinds of use would distort or weaken it
- how boundaries keep the work from turning into performance or control

### When to read it

Read this when you want a stronger sense of what keeps the practice intact, especially as it starts being shared, adapted, or applied in new places.
