This reading helps name the boundaries that keep the work recognizable as it
moves.

It is where the practice becomes more explicit about what it is committed to,
and what would make it stop being itself.

### What it helps you see

- what the practice depends on
- what kinds of use would distort or weaken it
- how boundary statements protect the work from turning into performance or control

### When to read it

Read this when you want a stronger sense of what keeps the practice intact,
especially when it starts being shared, adapted, or applied in new places.
