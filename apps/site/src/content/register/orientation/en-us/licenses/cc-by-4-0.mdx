## Summary

- share the work (copy it, redistribute it, or post it elsewhere)
- adapt the work (translate it, modify it, or reuse parts of it)

These permissions include commercial use.

You must:

- give appropriate credit to the author
- include a link to the license
- indicate whether you made changes

You may not:

- apply legal or technical restrictions that limit what the license permits others to do
