This site is a place to **read** the Practice of Clarity.

The goal is simple: help people explain their thinking in a way that other people can follow. Sometimes life moves fast. People make decisions quickly. Everyone ends up with different stories about what is happening and why. This practice is about making the "why" visible.

## How to use this site

This site has three ways to adjust what you see.

### Register (the site title)

Look at the site title at the top left. Next to "Practice of Clarity" you will see a small label: either **Practitioner** or **Orientation**. Click the title to switch between them.

**Orientation** is what you are reading right now. It uses everyday language and avoids jargon. **Practitioner** covers the same topics but is written for people who work in technology, design, or management. Both versions have the same pages and the same structure. Nothing is locked or hidden. You can switch freely at any time.

### Language

In the top right of the page (or in the mobile menu), there is a language selector. You can choose between English (US), English (UK), and German. Each language is written by a person, not auto-translated.

### Theme

Also in the top right, you can change how the site looks. There are dark and light options, and each comes in an "atmospheric" style with a soft blurred background or a "solid" style with a clean flat background. Pick whatever feels most comfortable to read.
