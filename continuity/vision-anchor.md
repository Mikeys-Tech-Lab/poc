## Purpose

This document preserves the rollout logic behind the Practice of Clarity
system.

It exists so the work does not get mistaken for:

- a content treadmill
- a publishing sprint
- a finished framework
- a fully public architecture before the public can hold it

The pacing is part of the architecture.

---

## What is being built

This is not just a set of articles.

It is a layered system that includes:

- seeds as structural anchors
- the Practice of Clarity as the method layer
- Acts as the first applied public bridge
- mandate lenses as contextual application packages
- continuity anchors that preserve direction over time

The public does not need to receive all of this at once.

That is deliberate.

---

## Why the rollout is slow

The system should not arrive before people can read it.

If the structural layers are released too early:

- they will be read as doctrine
- the method will be collapsed into slogans
- the deeper architecture will be treated as brand or ideology
- the public surface will become heavier than the audience can carry

The first task is not maximal publication.

The first task is to create enough reader capacity that deeper structures can
arrive without distortion.

---

## Rollout logic

### Phase 1: Public capacity first

Start with:

- the site
- the first articles
- the Acts as the readable bridge

This gives the public a way to encounter the work through use, not through
architecture diagrams.

### Phase 2: Structural anchors become inspectable

Once the public surface has enough coherence and reader trust, release the
seed pack and related root packages.

At this point, the system can begin to be inspected rather than merely read.

### Phase 3: Applied packages grow from real use

Mandate lenses should emerge from real pressure, not theory-first expansion.

They should stay:

- small
- inspectable
- tied to real workflows
- grounded in visible reasoning

### Phase 4: Broader federation remains conditional

Wider public constitutional or ecosystem layers should only appear after the
earlier layers have been used enough to survive contact with reality.

Nothing deep should be published only because it already exists internally.

---

## What must not happen

This system must not drift into:

- shipping faster than meaning can stabilize
- publishing structure for the sake of completeness
- making every internal layer public at once
- letting the audience mistake scaffolding for authority

The work is allowed to remain smaller in public than it already is internally.

That is not delay.

That is discipline.

---

## Immediate direction

The near-term public movement is now:

- keep the public article surface readable
- continue the Act sequence
- let Act IV open the node in a readable way
- expose the root canon as inspectable sibling packages
- make the first mandate lens and context seeder usable, not merely described

The root surface is being built here so rollout decisions can happen safely and
deliberately.

---

## Final constraint

If publication pressure starts to outrun understanding, slow down again.

The point is not to publish everything.

The point is to release what can still carry its trace.
